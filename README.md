# Windows-10-IoT-Enterprise-LTSC-2032-installation-guide
First of all, you will have to get a IoT LTSC 2032 (2021) ISO for this to work. This link gives you the ISO: https://archive.org/details/en-us_windows_10_iot_enterprise_ltsc_2021_x64_dvd_257ad90f_202301
Step 1:Use Rufus to burn the ISO to a USB. The USB must be at least of 8 GB. After burning it to the pen drive, boot your computer into the pen drive through the boot menu. Select the USB from the list and hit ENTER.
Step 2: Once your on the installation screen, go through it like normal.
Step 3: In the partition selector, choose or make a partition that is at least 100 GB. After that, click install.
Step 4: Go through the OOBE, after that you will be greeted onto the desktop.
Step 5: Open settings, install all the updates and then your PC is completly setup and provides updates till 2032.
